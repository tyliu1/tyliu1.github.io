I am a Ph.D. student of Future IoT Lab in the School of EECS at Peking University, advised by Prof. Lingyang Song, and co-advised by Prof. Kaigui Bian and Prof. Boya Di. My research interests lie in federated learning and reinforcement learning.

I received the Master's Degree from Peking University and Bachelor's Degree from Zhejiang University. I was ever an intern at Microsoft, Baidu, and MSRA.
